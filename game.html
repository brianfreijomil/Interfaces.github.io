4 en linea Messi vs Cristiano

"4 online Messi vs Cristiano" is a personalized game that refers to the historical "Connect 4". It is a game of reflection for children and adults that was released in 1974. Connect 4 became popular very quickly since the rules of the game are very simple, however the strategy to win requires experience on the part of the players. It is an excellent game to make your children's brain work. Similarly Connect 4 can be played on a sheet of paper with round tiles of different colors, this game will pass the time quickly if you have a long trip or boring courses. At RetroGames.com you can play 4 in a row Messi vs Cristiano with your friends or with the robot. Read our game strategies and be number one!

How to plat 4 en linea?

The rules of the 4 in line Messi vs Cristiano are very simple. It is always played between 2 players and on a 7x6 square board. On each turn, each player places a token of his character in a column and it falls to the first available space. The one who manages to place 4 tiles of the same character in a row horizontally, vertically or obliquely wins. If no one succeeds, the game ends in a draw.

Strategies to win in 4 en linea

To start the game well, it is important to place the maximum number of chips in the middle column, this will give you more chances to make a line in the following moves, and it will also give your opponent fewer chances to make a line. Then plan in advance a strategy where you trick your opponent into making a move that will allow you to win the game and move up the rankings!.

The double line

An easy way to trick your opponent in 4 en linea Messi vs Cristiano is to create two winning lines with just one tile short of the four in order to force your opponent to place a tile that will hit you. you the chance to line up four on the next play.

Video: https://youtu.be/G7YHiN39qS8

How to win against a beginner?

If you start the game of 4 in a row Messi vs Cristiano and your opponent is not very attentive, try to line up three of your chips on the bottom line very low. Your opponent will not be able to block your play on either the left or the right and in either case you will win.

Video: https://youtu.be/ZZh6Tb_3fbE

lock the columns

The following strategy must be well controlled if you want to quickly climb the rankings: try to block the columns by forcing your opponent to place a tile and you win on the next move (or this forms a double line). Try to place your chips by forcing your opponent to make moves he doesn't want to make and allowing you to plan the next moves to trap your opponent.
